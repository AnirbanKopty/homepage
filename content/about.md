Anirban Kopty is a fourth-year research scholar at IUCAA, working in gravitational wave data analysis under the supervision of Prof. Sanjit Mitra. In particular, he has devised a low-latency method for searching for strongly lensed gravitational wave signals that have not yet been observed. Additionally, he works on fast parameter estimation techniques and tests of GR. He has a background in Physics and is interested in current and upcoming challenges in gravitational wave data analysis.
